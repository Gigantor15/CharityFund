Charity Events

Services

We provide a platform to the people from independent users to widely-known charitable organizations to advertise their cause and receive donations from supportive users. Also enabling the organizations to events in which can encourage people to donate within a timely manner.

Heading

Subscribed users have the ability to choose a charity of their choice, receive updates and invites to upcoming events. This enables charities to have a personal connection with their supporters and makes their supporters feel as if they are playing active roles in furthering their charities future for the embetterment of their individual cause..
